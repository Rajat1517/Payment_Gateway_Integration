Crowdfunding is a quick and easy way to raise money using the internet and social media. Using an online fundraiser page, you can raise large amounts through donations from across the world to help any social, medical, personal or creative need.Campaigners have used crowdfunding to pay for critical medical treatments such as cancer care or transplant, support disaster relief work, help a non-profit or a social cause and even fund personal needs such as education or businesses. More and more Indians are opting for crowdfunding over traditional financial options such as loans or insurance. Over 70% of Indians are either not medically insured or are grossly under-insured. This means their insurance is rarely able to cover the steep medical costs of critical treatments like cancer or prenatal care.
